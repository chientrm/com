# YouWave Privacy Policy

## Introduction

Welcome to the YouWave app for Android!

This is an Android app developed by chientrm. The app is available on Google Play.

As a loyal Android user, I take care of user privacy carefully.

## Manifest Permissions required by the app

| Permission                                  | Why it is required                                                   |
| ------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| `android.permission.INTERNET`               | Retrieving, uploading, and playing audio streams online              |
| `android.permission.READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE`  | Uploading audio from user's devices to the cloud.                    |
| `android.permission.WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` | Download audio from the cloud to local storage for offline playback. |
| `android.permission.READ_MEDIA_IMAGES`      | Upload image as avatar.                                              |
| `android.permission.READ_MEDIA_VIDEO`       | Preview audio before uploading.                                      |
| `android.permission.READ_MEDIA_AUDIO`       | Preview audio before uploading.                                      |
| `android.permission.FOREGROUND_SERVICE`     | Playing audio in the background without required user intervention.  |
| `android.permission.POST_NOTIFICATIONS`     | Upload/download status notification.                                 |

## Data privacy

YouWave Android doesn't collect any of your personal data. Personal data are securely handled by Google Firebase.

Anonymous user can use most of the features provided by YouWave.

In order to upload audio, user need to register a unique `handle` with YouWave which is the only personal information that is exposed to the internet.

YouWave only stores the processed version of your uploaded assets. Intermediate resources are removed
once the processing is done.

Users can choose to help us with the app Crashlytics and diagnosis by sending app info and performance. The data sent is
handled by Firebase Crashlytics and Performance.

We intentionally collect your app interactions so that we can make the app more eligible and usable which would help
improve user experience.

We intentionally collect your search history so that we can personalize your user experience.

Users can always review, enable or disable app interactions and search history data collection.

By removing your audio from the app, your audio file and related information are no longer available on our server.

By choosing to delete data, all personal data and assets are completely removed from our servers.

All private information is not exposed or shared with any other users or third-party organization. If you find yourself or other people's information exposed, please contact us using the `Contact information` provided at the bottom of this document so that we can fix the issue.

## Changes

This privacy policy may be updated along with the development and evolution of the app. Updates will be listed in changelogs.

## Contact information

If you would like to contact us to understand more about this Policy or wish to contact us concerning any matter
relating to individual rights and your Personal Information, you may send an email to [admin@chientrm.com](mailto:admin@chientrm.com).
